Our Story

Manifesting the Power of God in Nellmapius since 2014

Welcome to MPGM

MPGM (Manifesting the Power of God Ministries) was founded in 2014 by Bishop Sekhaolele with a vision to bring hope, healing, and transformation to the Nellmapius community through the power of God's word.

What began as a small gathering of believers has grown into a vibrant ministry impacting hundreds of lives each week through worship services, community outreach, and discipleship programs.

Our mission is to manifest God's power through Spirit-filled worship, biblical teaching, and compassionate service to our community.
