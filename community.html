Our Community Programs

Food Security Program

We provide monthly food parcels to vulnerable families in Nellmapius and surrounding areas. Our feeding scheme serves hot meals to children after school.

Learn More

Education Support

We supply school uniforms, stationery, and after-school tutoring to help children from disadvantaged backgrounds succeed academically.

Learn More

Skills Development

Monthly workshops teach practical skills like sewing, gardening, and basic computer literacy to empower community members economically.

Learn More

Counseling Services

Our trained counselors provide free trauma counseling, marriage counseling, and addiction recovery support.

Learn More

Volunteer With Us

Join our team of dedicated volunteers who are making a difference in our community. Whether you can give a few hours a week or just help with special events, your time and talents are valuable.

  • Home repairs
  • Event coordination
